As per today’s scenario the reach of notifications from college management to students is very important. We are sending the notifications through circulars and phone messages. The students are not able to get the notifications because of reasons like absent to the college, not in the class at that time, switched off mobile, improper network, etc.… This being a small issue, but it is a major one in situations like notification regarding exams, holidays, attendance etc. We need to maintain a proper platform for the students to keep updated regarding all the notifications and updates from college.


The application provides information about the college, management system, courses that are offered and placement information. Besides, it also provides details about the student such as attendance, academic performance and fee payment. Through this application students can also access online materials. Registered users (Students, Faculty) will get notifications about college cultural events, workshops, seminars, exams etc. Finally, this application also includes college gallery and contact information.
